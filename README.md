Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Swiggy's transactional data to derive actionable business insights. Using Advanced Microsoft Excel, I transformed a complex dataset into a high-impact interactive dashboard. The analysis covers sales performance, customer satisfaction metrics, and geographical distribution, providing a 360-degree view of Swiggy’s operations.
Key Objectives
Performance Tracking: Monitoring revenue growth and order volume fluctuations over time.
Customer Behavior: Analyzing rating patterns and dietary preferences (Veg vs. Non-Veg).
Geographical Insights: Identifying high-performing regions and cities to assist in strategic expansion.
Efficiency Metrics: Calculating the Average Order Value (AOV) to understand consumer spending power.
Data Engineering & Workflow
To ensure the accuracy of the dashboard, I followed a structured data pipeline:
Data Cleaning: Utilized Power Query to remove duplicates, handle null values in the 'Ratings' column, and standardize city names.
Feature Engineering: Created calculated columns for "Day Name," "Month," and "Quarter" to enable granular time-series analysis.
Analytical Processing: Leveraged Pivot Tables and complex Excel formulas (IFS, VLOOKUP, SUMIFS) to aggregate data across multiple dimensions.
Dashboard Highlights (Insights Gained)
Sales Momentum: The Monthly Sales Trend reveals peak performance periods, helping identify the impact of seasonal promotions.
Dietary Split: A significant 67% of orders are Non-Vegetarian, indicating a clear market preference that can drive inventory and partner onboarding strategies.
Regional Heatmap: The geographical visualization highlights that while sales are widespread, specific states contribute to the bulk of the ₹ 53.01M total revenue.
Customer Trust: Maintaining an Average Rating of 4.34 across 5.59M reviews indicates high service reliability and brand loyalty.
Business Impact
This dashboard serves as a decision-support tool for stakeholders to:
Optimize Marketing: Allocate budget to top-performing cities like Mumbai and Bangalore.
Improve Operations: Address "Daily Sales Trends" to manage delivery partner requirements during peak days (e.g., weekends).
Strategy Planning: Use the Average Order Value (₹ 268.51) to design upselling and cross-selling strategies.
