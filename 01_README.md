Swiggy Sales & Customer Ratings Analytics Dashboard


problem statment 

The management team required a consolidated view of operations to answer critical questions.
Sales Momentum: Which months and days show the highest order volume?
Customer Satisfaction:  How are ratings distributed across different food categories (Veg vs. Non-Veg)?
Regional Performance:  Which cities (Mumbai, Bengaluru, etc.) are driving the most revenue?
Order Value:   What is the Average Order Value (AOV), and is it consistent across different quarters?
Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Swiggy's transactional data to derive actionable business insights. Using Advanced Microsoft Excel, I transformed a complex dataset into a high-impact interactive dashboard. The analysis covers sales performance, customer satisfaction metrics, and geographical distribution, providing a 360-degree view of Swiggy’s operations.

Objectives

Performance Tracking:
            Monitoring revenue growth and order volume fluctuations over time.
Customer Behavior: 
            Analyzing rating patterns and dietary preferences (Veg vs. Non-Veg).
Geographical Insights: 
             Identifying high-performing regions and cities to assist in strategic expansion.
Efficiency Metrics:
            Calculating the Average Order Value (AOV) to understand consumer spending power.
Workflow :
            To ensure the accuracy of the dashboard.
Data Cleaning:
            Utilized Power Query to remove duplicates, handle null values in the 'Ratings' column, and standardize city                  names.

Analytical Processing: 
              Leveraged Pivot Tables and complex Excel formulas (IFS, VLOOKUP, SUMIFS) to aggregate data across multiple dimensions.
              
Dashboard Highlights 

Sales Momentum : 
             The Monthly Sales Trend reveals peak performance periods, helping identify the impact of seasonal promotions.
Dietary Split:            
             A significant 67% of orders are Non-Vegetarian, indicating a clear market preference that can drive inventory                and partner onboarding strategies.
Regional Heatmap: 
             The geographical visualization highlights that while sales are widespread, specific states contribute to the                 bulk of  total revenue.
Customer Trust: 
             Maintaining an Average Rating of 4.34 across 5.59M reviews indicates high service reliability and brand loyalty.

Business Impact

This dashboard serves as a decision-support tool for stakeholders to
Optimize Marketing: 
                  Allocate budget to top-performing cities like Mumbai and Bangalore.
Improve Operations:
                   Address Daily Sales Trend  to manage delivery partner requirements during peak days.
Strategy Plannin :
                   Use the Average Order Value (₹ 268.51) to design upselling and cross-selling strategies.
